Swallow

Description

On one hand, we have eba, which is basically cassava flour that thickened by addition of hot water with consistent stirring. On the other hand, we have egusi soup, which is made by grinding melon seed and adding pumpkin leaf.

Ingredients

  • Garri
  • Water
  • Melon seed
  • Vegetable
  • condiments
  • fish

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Pour hot water in a bowl
  3. Pour the garri genlty into the bowl
  4. And stir with a spatula
  5. Voila, eba is ready
